Our story

SimpleTax started with a simple question. Why?

Why is preparing my tax return so complicated? Why is tax software so slow and hard to use? Why is it so expensive? We think this simple question is why over 6 million tax returns are still done on paper and why more than half of Canadians turn to a tax preparer each year, even for the simplest returns.

We believe there is a better way to do your taxes.

We believe taxes should be simple enough that you can do them yourself. We believe you shouldn't have to pay to do your taxes. We believe you're entitled to your full refund, as quickly as possible. We believe too much paper is wasted every year.

In short, we believe you deserve better. That's why we're building the best tax software in Canada.
